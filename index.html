ABOUT TOPPLAYLIST

TopPlaylist is an open-source web application to track the user's most played tracks and albums, and creates a playlist around it.

It also creates playlist for recommendations based on their music tastes based on their previously played tracks and artists.

You don't have to wait every year for wrapped anymore!
